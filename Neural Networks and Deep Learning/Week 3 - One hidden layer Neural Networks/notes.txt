	Forward propagation through layers
	-	Layer 1:		Z[1] = W[1]*X[1] + b[1]
						A[1] = sigmoid(Z[1])
	
		Layer 2:		Z[2] = W[2]*A[1] + b[2]
						A[2] = sigmoid(Z[2])
						...
		Layer i:		Z[i] = W[i]*A[i-1] + b[i]
						A[i] = sigmoid(Z[i])
						L(A[i], Y) = -(1/m)*sum((Y*log(A[i].T)) + (1 - Y)*log(1 - A[i].T))
